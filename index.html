UCSD Raas Ruckus is a competitive collegiate team dedicated to maintaining and expressing an integral part of Indian culture: dance. Specializing in Raas-Garba, a traditional dance style that originates from the Indian state of Gujarat, Ruckus travels yearly performing in some of the nation’s largest cities and competing in the national circuit . Since its founding in 2003, Raas Ruckus has featured some of UCSD campus’ most diverse dancers and leaders, consistently assembling a team of talent and turn-up .
